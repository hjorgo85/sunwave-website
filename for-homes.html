Where Infrared Fits in a Swiss Home

Most homes don't need a whole new heating system — they need the one room that's always cold to finally be warm. Here's where SunWave Ceramica fits.

The room your heating doesn't reach

Every Swiss home has one — the home office a few degrees behind, the north-facing bedroom, the converted attic, the bathroom that's cold until the boiler's been running for an hour. Re-plumbing a single room for central heating is disruptive and expensive for what it solves.

A SunWave Ceramica panel solves it directly. Mount it on the wall, plug it in (or have it hardwired by an electrician), and that room is warm in minutes — independent of the rest of your heating system. No re-plumbing, no renovation, no permits.

A natural partner for solar PV

If your home has solar panels, infrared is one of the simplest ways to put daytime generation to direct use — switch a panel on while the sun's out and your meter is running backwards anyway. No batteries or special wiring required, just an ordinary Swiss socket.

Health advantages in Swiss homes

Swiss residential buildings are among the best-sealed in Europe — excellent for energy efficiency, but it means anything that off-gasses indoors lingers longer. Infrared involves no combustion and no air movement to stir up dust or allergens. Fraunhofer WKI measured TVOC emissions of just 0.043 mg/m³ — far below regulatory limits, with no carcinogenic compounds detected. That matters most in tightly sealed Swiss Minergie buildings.

From Decision to Warm Rooms in a Day

Installing SunWave Ceramica is simpler and faster than replacing a boiler — typically completed in a single day by a certified Swiss electrician.

  • 1

    Pick your cold rooms

    Start with the rooms that need it most — most homes begin with one or two panels. Check our room-by-room guide or contact us with your room sizes and we'll help you work out the right number and placement.

  • 2

    Choose your finish

    Select from nine porcelain finishes to match your interior. All panels are the same 60×120 cm size with identical performance. You can mix finishes between rooms.

  • 3

    Order and delivery (3–5 working days)

    Panels ship from our Swiss warehouse. CHF 35 delivery anywhere in Switzerland (Swiss Post PostPac Priority). Each panel arrives packaged to prevent transit damage — replacement guaranteed if damaged on arrival.

  • 4

    Installation (plug-in or hardwired)

    Plug-in installation (standard Swiss T13 socket) requires no electrician. Hardwired installation (tidier, no visible cable) takes a certified Swiss electrician approximately 45–60 minutes per panel. A typical 3-panel apartment is done in a morning.

  • 5

    Set the thermostat and enjoy

    Programme your smart thermostat schedule — higher during occupied hours, lower overnight. Many homeowners see the heating bill difference from the first monthly statement.
