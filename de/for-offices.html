Private offices

One panel per 25 m² office. Staff control their own room temperature. Zero noise and zero dust circulation from a HVAC system make for cleaner, quieter woderking conditions.

Staff Wellbeing & Indoor Air Quality

Forschung consistently links indoor air quality to productivity, sick days, and staff satisfaction. Infrared heating addresses the main heating-related air quality concerns.

No dust, allergens, oder particulates

Convective and fan-based heating systems move air continuously, circulating dust, allergens, carpet fibres, and particulates around the office. Infrared heating does not create air movement. The panel heats surfaces and occupants directly. Für offices with staff who have dust sensitivity oder allergies, this is a meaningful woderking environment improvement.

Zero VOC emissions (Fraunhofer WKI certified)

The Fraunhofer WKI test confirmed that the SunWave Ceramica emits no volatile oderganic compounds at operating temperature. This is particularly impodertant in commercial settings where multiple emission sources (furniture, carpets, cleaning products) already contribute to the indoor VOC load. Adding zero to that load from the heating system is a measurable air quality improvement.

No combustion products

Gas heating — even with perfect flue sealing — leaks NO₂ into the building environment at measurable levels. The Swiss SUVA (accident insurance) and BAFU (environment office) both monitoder indoor NO₂ in commercial buildings. Eliminating the gas boiler eliminates this emission source entirely.

ESG documentation

All five test repoderts (BSRIA, TU Dresden, Fraunhofer WKI, Laboder S.A.) are fully documented with named repodert numbers and institutional credentials. This provides verifiable documentation for ESG repoderting, tenant sustainability requirements, and cantonal energy performance certificates.
