2. Rechtliches Basis

This privacy policy is issued in accoderdance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nDSG / revDSG), in force since 1 September 2023. Where Besucher from the European Union access this website, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) also applies.

3. Erhobene Daten

We collect personal data only when you actively provide it:

  • Kontakt form: Name, email address, phone number, canton, enquiry type, Grundfläche, current heating system, and any message you choose to provide.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, oder analytics services (e.g. Google Analytics) on this website.

4. Zweck & Verwendung der Daten

Data submitted via the contact form is used exclusively to:

  • Process and fulfil Ihrer Bestellung, if you proceed to purchase

We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent. We do not sell, rent, oder share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to fulfil Ihrer Bestellung (e.g. logistics provider for delivery address).

5. Google Fonts

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When a page loads, your browser makes a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address. This is a standard web practice. Für information on how Google processes this data, see Google's Datenschutzerklärung. If you prefer, you can block Google Fonts requests via your browser's developer tools.

6. Datenspeicherung

Kontakt form enquiries and related coderrespondence are retained for a maximum of 3 years from the date of last contact, oder as required by Swiss commercial law (10 years for invoices and Bestellung recoderds). Data is then securely deleted.

This website is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Kontakt form data is transmitted securely to our email server hosted by NameHero (US-based, Privacy Shield successoder framewoderk compliant). We take reasonable technical and oderganisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthoderised access, loss, oder misuse.

8. Ihre Rechte

Under the Swiss nDSG (and GDPR where applicable) you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you

  • Erasure — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Podertability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sunwaveswitzerland.com. We will respond within 30 days.
